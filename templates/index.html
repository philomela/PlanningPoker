What is Planning Poker?

Planning poker is a problem assessment technique based on team discussion of each problem. Each participant anonymously evaluates the problem by voting with a scorecard. Then the cards are revealed and the median for each problem is calculated.

How it works?

1. Register or login and click the button create a room.

2. Fill out the form. Come up with a name for the meeting, and plan tasks that will be evaluated by your team members.

3. Share the link to the room invitations to your team and play planning poker while evaluating tasks.
